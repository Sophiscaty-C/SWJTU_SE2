Li calls for cooperation with Ghana in human resources, agriculture - World - Chinadaily.com.cn
Premier Li Keqiang called on Saturday to promote cooperation in production capacity, human resources, agriculture and fishery with Ghana.
He spoke when meeting with the visiting Ghana President Nana Akufo-Addo, who came to Beijing for a state visit and to participate in the 2018 Beijing Summit of the Forum on China-Africa Cooperation.
Ghana is an influential nation in western Africa and a vital partner for China on the continent, Li said. President Xi Jinping held a productive meeting with Akufo-Addo to plan for the future development of bilateral relations, he said.
China would like to work with the African partner in consolidating the promising momentum for a comprehensive and fast development of bilateral cooperation, Li said.
Li also called on Ghana to improve its legislation and create a more fair environment for investment and businesses.
Akufo-Addo said Ghana has benefited a lot from its cooperation with China, and both countries have strongly promoted infrastructure, including highways, railways and telecommunications. Ghana is ready to sign a bilateral investment treaty with China as soon as possible to bring relations to a higher level, he added.
