Sixty Nigerian engineers trained in China - World - Chinadaily.com.cn
Nigerian Ajayi Adeniyi Olugbenga is enthusiastic of learning advanced manufacturing and maintenance technology in the industrial city of Anshan, Liaoning province.
The 41-year-old engineer is one of a 60-member team from Nigeria that in August came to Northeast China's Liaoning province for a half-year's training of theory, transformer design and equipment maintenance.
In order to upgrade Nigeria's power grid, Liaoning Huaye Group and China Great Wall Industry Group Cooperation jointly undertook the construction of Nigeria electrical industry park.
The $300-million project consists of three transformer plants and one high-voltage power testing center.
