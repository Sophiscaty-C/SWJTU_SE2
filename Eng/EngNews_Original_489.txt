Video: China-Africa cooperation at high speed - World - Chinadaily.com.cn
Have you ever seen elephants, zebras, lions, antelopes and giraffes roam freely alongside a railway? This is the Mombasa-Nairobi Railway, the symbolic fruit of China-Africa friendly cooperation proposed by China at the Johannesburg Summit of the Forum on China-Africa Cooperation in 2015. The Beijing Summit of the forum will be held on September 3 to 4 to forge an even better China-Africa community of shared future.
