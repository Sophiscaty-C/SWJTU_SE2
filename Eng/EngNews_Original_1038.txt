We oppose politicization and stigmatization of COVID-19: Xi - Chinadaily.com.cn
BEIJING - We oppose politicization and stigmatization of COVID-19, Chinese President Xi Jinping said on Wednesday.
"We oppose racial discrimination and ideological bias. We stand firm for equity and justice in the world," Xi said in his keynote speech at the Extraordinary China-Africa Summit on Solidarity against COVID-19 held via video link.
