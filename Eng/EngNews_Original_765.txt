Hannah Getachew invites young people, mom to Changsha - World - Chinadaily.com.cn
The International Youth Forum on Creativity and Heritage along the Silk Roads will kick off in Changsha, Central China's Hunan province, on March 31, 2019.
Hannah Getachew, who received a reply from President Xi Jinping when she wrote to him last year, wants young people around the world to come and experience the lovely and exciting Changsha. She went to Changsha for the youth forum last year, and was captivated by the modern and traditional city with a long history and rich culture. She wrote a letter to her mom, hoping she also could come to Changsha and experience the charm of the city.
