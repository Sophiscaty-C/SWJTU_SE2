69-year-old US Army veteran shows 'patriotism' scars - World - Chinadaily.com.cn
A video of an Asian American military veteran taking his shirt off during a town hall meeting to show his &quot;patriotism&quot; went viral. Lee Wong, the chair of the township Board of Trustees in West Chester, Ohio, — a position equivalent to mayor, who served in the US Army for 20 years, shared his views with China Daily about racial discrimination.
