China to send medical teams to Djibouti, Cote d'Ivoire - Chinadaily.com.cn
BEIJING - The Chinese government has decided to send teams of medical experts to Djibouti and Cote d'Ivoire to help the countries fight the COVID-19 pandemic, Foreign Ministry spokesperson Hua Chunying announced Friday.
The teams are organized by the National Health Commission and selected by health commissions of Sichuan province and Tianjin municipality, respectively, according to the spokesperson.
