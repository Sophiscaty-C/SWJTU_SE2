Nine Chinese seamen abducted - World - Chinadaily.com.cn
Nine Chinese and eight Ukrainian seamen were abducted on Thursday when two merchant vessels came under attack in Cameroonian waters in the Gulf of Guinea, sources said Friday, Aug 16.
&quot;Seventeen Chinese and Ukrainians were kidnapped... (of whom) 9 (are) Chinese who were abducted on one of the ships,&quot; an official in the port of Douala told AFP.
