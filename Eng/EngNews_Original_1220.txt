US climate envoy John Kerry to meet Chinese counterpart - World - Chinadaily.com.cn
United States special climate envoy John Kerry is going to talk with his Chinese counterpart Xie Zhenhua during a visit to Shanghai from Wednesday to Saturday, according to a media release from the Ministry of Ecology and Environment.
The visit is at the invitation of the Chinese side. The duo will exchange views over Sino-US climate cooperation and the COP 26 United Nations climate change conference to be held in Glasgow in the United Kingdom in November, according to the ministry.
